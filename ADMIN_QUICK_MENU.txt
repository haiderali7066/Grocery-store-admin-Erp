================================================================================
                            ROLE-BASED ACCESS
================================================================================

ADMIN (Full Access)
  ✅ All 15 modules
  ✅ Create, edit, delete everything
  ✅ Manage wallets & investments
  ✅ Manage staff & roles
  ✅ View all reports
  ✅ Change FBR & system settings

MANAGER (Limited Access)
  ✅ Products (create/edit only)
  ✅ Inventory & Purchases
  ✅ Order Processing
  ✅ POS Billing
  ✅ POS Reports
  ❌ Cannot delete products
  ❌ Cannot manage wallets
  ❌ Cannot see full profit/loss
  ❌ Cannot manage staff
  ❌ Cannot change FBR or investment

ACCOUNTANT (Reports Only)
  ✅ Reports & Analytics
  ✅ Financial reports
  ✅ Profit & Loss
  ❌ Cannot modify anything

STAFF (POS Only)
  ✅ POS Billing
  ✅ Basic inventory view
  ❌ Cannot modify anything except POS
